# **App Name**: PrimeForage Website

## Core Features:

- Company Overview: Display PrimeForage's mission, vision, and values.
- Services Showcase: Highlight PrimeForage's key services with detailed descriptions and visuals.
- Team Introduction: Introduce the team members with profiles and expertise.
- Contact Form: Enable users to submit inquiries and contact the company.
- Client Testimonials: Showcase positive feedback and success stories from PrimeForage's clients.
- Blog/News Section: Share company updates, industry insights, and relevant articles.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep forest green (#228B22) to evoke a sense of growth, stability, and reliability.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a soft, neutral backdrop that enhances readability.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFD700) used sparingly to draw attention to important elements, adding a touch of sophistication and energy.
- Font Pairing: 'Alegreya' for headings, bringing an elegant, intellectual feel, paired with 'PT Sans' for body text, which has a modern, yet warm and readable feel.
- Use simple, clean icons that complement the overall aesthetic and represent the company's services.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout to ensure the site is well-organized and easy to navigate.
- Incorporate subtle animations and transitions to add visual interest and improve user experience.
